About-Farsh E AZA

Spreading Message of Islam Prophet Muhammad (pbuh)

Believing that Allah had chosen him as his messenger Muhammad began to preach what Allah had revealed to him. The simple and clear-cut message of Islam, that there is no God but Allah, and that life should be lived in complete submission to the will of Allah, was attractive to many people, and they flocked to hear it.

Who we are!

Farsh E AZA is a voluntary, non-profit, non-governmental,non-political, social welfare and social development based organization for the service of distressed humanity in Bangladesh. Determination,diversification and devotion towards Prophet Muhammad (pbuh) and his household Ahlul Bayt (as) are the driving force behind to form Farsh E AZA .
